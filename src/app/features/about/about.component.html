HELLO!

My name is Pola. I want to study game development to deepen my skills and because of my strong interest in narrative-driven and atmospheric games. My strengths are persistence, attention to detail and quick learning. Creating interactive experiences allows me to combine programming with my artistic skills. I especially enjoy fantasy worlds, cozy pixel-art games, and narrative-driven projects with deep lore.
I am a self-taught artist, working mainly in digital semi-realistic and anime art styles using Clip Studio Paint. Recently, I have also been exploring pixel art and simple animations in Aseprite. I am currently learning Unity 2D and C#, and I am interested in expanding my skills in game design and programming.
In addition to IT, I have a background in mechatronics and basic knowledge of electronics. I have experience working with Arduino and Raspberry Pi, which has helped me understand the connection between software and hardware. In the future, I would like to learn more about 3D modeling in Blender and Unreal Engine. I also have basic experience with front-end development.

Hope you enjoy my portfolio!
